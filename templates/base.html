Store Manifesto

At Derek's Obscure Books, we sell books that nobody read then and nobody reads now. This is what made Naturalism one of the shortest-lived literary movements since the dawn of the written word. By selling these books, we fulfill two life-long dreams: Selling books and never having to speak to customers. This truly is the greatest place on earth.
